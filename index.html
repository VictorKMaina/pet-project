This is Bruno.

Bruno was born as a stray on the streets of Mombasa in 2019. His mother left him alone in a box on the streets. Luckily for him, he was found by a kind family that took him in as one of their own. Now, Bruno lives a life most dogs could only dream of.

Bruno loves his chew toys. His favorite is a little rubber duck that makes a squeaky sound. Because he is extremly playful, Bruno hardly ever sleeps.

Bruno is also one of those canines that hates his dog food. But put him in front of some fresh tuna, and he will simply go crazy!

There are many other animals like Bruno. Most of them die on the street, or end in animal shelters where they're treated poorly. So the next time you see a poor, young puppy shivering out in the cold, don't hesitate to lend a hand.
